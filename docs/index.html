Introduction¶

Dataset Description¶

In this project we'll be analyzing data associated with 10,000 movies collected from TMDB, where each row corresponds to a movie that contains data about the movie, some of these data incude:

  • Budget
  • Revenue
  • Cast
  • Director
  • Original title
  • Average rating

Question(s) for Analysis¶

  1. What is the popularity of genres across the years?
  2. What properties are associated with high revenue?
  3. Who are the top earning directors?
  4. Who are the top earning actors?
  5. Is budget associated with high revenue?
  6. Is runtime associated with high revenue?
  7. Is rating associated with high revenue?
  8. Is popularity associated with high revenue?
